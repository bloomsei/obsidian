## Service Provider

1. Produce a lot fast. Don’t think too much. Don’t care about quality too much. **Delivering is all**
2. **You are a service provider.** Act accordingly
    1. Do what is ordered
    2. Provide alternatives, flag risks, don’t question
    3. Don’t do much of what you were not assigned
